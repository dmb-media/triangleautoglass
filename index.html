Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

ADAS Recalibration Specialists

Modern vehicles are equipped with Advanced Driver Assistance Systems that rely on precisely calibrated cameras and sensors. After a windshield replacement, these systems must be recalibrated to ensure they function correctly and keep you safe on the road.

Our technicians are certified ADAS recalibration specialists. We use professional-grade equipment to ensure your vehicle's safety systems are properly calibrated after every installation.

Our Promise

Warranty

Triangle Auto Glass guarantees all windshields and back glass installations against leaks or defects in the workmanship or materials for the period of time the original customer owns or leases the vehicle. This warranty is valid unless the leak is the result of a defect in the vehicle. These include, but are not limited to, rust damage, separated body seams, firewall damages, etc.

This warranty will become void if damage or breakage occurs due to accident, misuse, neglect, abuse, theft, vandalism, Act of God, or self-inflicted damage.
